Vasistha

(Sanskrit: वसिष्ठ, IAST: vasiṣṭha)

Some highlights of Vasistha

  • one of the Saptarishis (Seven Great Sages Rishi) in the seventh
  • one of 9 Prajapatis is credited as the chief author of Mandala 7 of the Rigveda
  • a peace-loving, selfless, intelligent and great Rishi
  • possessing 20 "kala's" (divine arts) and had complete knowledge of the whole cosmos and the god
  • The tale of King Dileepa
